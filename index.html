About

Stell Technology commenced business in May 2004 as the pioneer ICT distributor in Azerbaijan and currently represents the world’s leading manufacturers: APC, Cisco, Canon, Epson, NetApp, HPE, HP and etc. Our vision is to become the key driver of technology revolution across Azerbaijan, while our mission is to make ICT products accessible, affordable and usable across country. Stell Technology offers a wide range of equipment and services such as servers, integrated systems, software, networking, storage and etc. All the equipment supplied by the company is covered by warranty and support from a reliable service center.
